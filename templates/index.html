TSH_Level

TSH (Thyroid-Stimulating Hormone) is a hormone produced by the pituitary gland in the brain. It regulates the thyroid gland, which controls metabolism, energy levels, and overall body function.
Ranges :
Normal -> 0.4 - 4.0 Healthy thyroid function Low TSH -> Below 0.4 Possible hyperthyroidism (overactive thyroid) High TSH -> Above 4.0 Possible hypothyroidism (underactive thyroid) Click for more
